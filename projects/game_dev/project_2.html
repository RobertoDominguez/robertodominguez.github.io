VR Online Store

June, 2021

Developed an immersive virtual reality shopping experience using Unity, designed for a high-profile investment showcase. The app enabled users to:

Explore products in 3D interactive environments with realistic spatial audio

View dynamic pricing and promotional information in VR

Navigate the virtual store using hand-tracking controllers

Developing this VR app deepened my Unity 3D skills, taught me to optimize for VR hardware, and showed me how to create immersive commerce experiences that drive engagement.
